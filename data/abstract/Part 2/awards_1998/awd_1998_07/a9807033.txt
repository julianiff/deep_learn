              Quinn  9807033    This task order provides technical and administrative support
              for processing proposals received in the Foundation-wide Project, Professional
              Opportunities for Women in Research and Education.  Activities include initial
              data-entry, spreadsheet preparation and jacket preparation for proposals
              received by the 9 December deadline.  Initial data entry will include assembly
              of proposal jackets, entry of data onto the NSF mainframe system, and mailing
              of acknowledge cards and attachments.
