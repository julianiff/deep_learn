Abstract    :
                 This award provides partial support for a Mind, Brain and Behavior Symposium
              Series at the 1998 annual convention of the American Psychological Association.
              Organized jointly by APA's  Division 6 (Behavioral Neuroscience and Comparative
              Psychology) and Division 3 (Experimental Psychology), the series is designed as
              an educational opportunity for students and nonspecialists.  The symposia will
              offer presentations by prominent researchers in the fields of vision,
              perception, attention, memory, language, decision and choice, learning,
              hormones and behavior, and ingestive behavior.
