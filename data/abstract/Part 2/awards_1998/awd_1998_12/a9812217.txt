Abstract    :
                   The 1998 Symposium on Weak and Electromagnetic  Interactions in Nuclei, to
              be held June 14-19, 1998, will  focus on searches for new physics at low and
              medium  energies, including theoretical studies in these areas.  In  addition,
              selected topics in the physics of the `standard  model` will be included. 
              Searches for new physics at high  energy facilities, and topics in nuclear and
              particle  astrophysics will also be discussed.       The interaction between
              nuclear, atomic, and particle  physicists which will take place at the
              Symposium should  prove very scientifically fruitful.
