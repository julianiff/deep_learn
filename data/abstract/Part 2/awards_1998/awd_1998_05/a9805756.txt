Abstract    :
              This award provides travel support to allow members of the History of Science
              Society, the Philosophy of Science Society, the Society for the History of
              Technology, and the Society for Social Studies of Science to attend an
              international Science and Technology Studies conference in Japan.  The
              conference will be held in Tokyo, Kyoto, and Hiroshima from March 16 through
              March 22, 1998.  The purpose of the award is to provide travel assistance,
              particularly to more junior scholars, allowing them to attend this important
              meeting (which will be the first of its kind in Japan).
