Abstract    :
                   Theoretical studies of the collisions of Positrons with  simple atomic
              systems are undertaken to enhance our  understanding of the interaction of
              three charged particles.  The problem is particularly difficult due to the real
              or  virtual formation of Positronium and the results of the  calculations are
              of importance to our understanding of  positron annihilation in solar flares
              and the galactic  center.
