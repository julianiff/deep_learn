              Abstract                                                                     
              Funds will be used to study array configurations and alternative  paraboloid
              designs for the Square Kilometer Array, attempting to  take advantage of mass
              production economies.  A series of  workshops, including relevant expertise
              from several MIT  departments and Haystack Observatory, will be organized to
              carry  out the study.
