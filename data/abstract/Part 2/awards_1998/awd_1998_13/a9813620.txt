The objective is to provide partial support for travel of
              students from U.S. universities attending the 1998 American Control Conference
              (ACC) to be held in Philadelphia, PA, USA, during June 24-26, 1998.  The
              American Control Conference (ACC) is one of the largest control engineering
              conference in USA.  The 1998 ACC will cover a wide range of topics dealing with
              theory and applications of control engineering.  Typically, the topics covered
              by the conference include control theory, robotics, manufacturing, guidance and
              flight control power systems, process control and control engineering
              education.
