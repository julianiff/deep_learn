Abstract    :
              This award is made under the high performance connections portion of ANIR's
              "Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 98-102. It provides partial
              support for two years for a OC-3 connection to the vBNS. Applications include
              data analysis, data storage, device control and monitoring, digital libraries,
              teleconferencing; distance learning and remote experimentation. Collaborating
              institutions include the Department of Energy's Thomas Jefferson National
              Accelerator Facility (Jefferson Lab), Continuous Electron Beam Accelerator
              Facility (CEBAF), Baptist Health Systems of South Florida, NASA, and the Air
              Force Research Lab.
