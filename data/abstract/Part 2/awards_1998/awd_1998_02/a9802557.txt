Abstract    :
                   The objectives of this research are to achieve a  quantitative
              understanding of the interaction of radiation  with atomic systems.  The
              emphasis is on the quantitative  treatment of multi-electron processes using a
              B-spline based  configuration interaction approach.  Resonance energies and 
              widths and photoionization cross sections will be computed  for a variety of
              ground and excited states atomic species.
