Abstract    :
                   The `Light-Cone QCD Workhsop` will take place July 6-  15, 1998 in St.
              Petersburg, Russia.  This workshop will  consider how a broad range of complex
              phenomena exhibited by  the strong nuclear force may be understood in terms of
              the  theory known as Quantum Chromodynamics (QCD).  These  phenomena include
              the confinement of quarks within nucleons,  and processes initiated by a quark
              which is inside a nucleon  and carries only a small fraction of the momentum
              possessed  by the nucleon as a whole.  The workshop will facilitate the 
              exchange of ideas crucial to progress in the field.
