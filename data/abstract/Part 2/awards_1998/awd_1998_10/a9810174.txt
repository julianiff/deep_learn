Abstract    :
              This is a Professional Development Proposal that would allow the investigator to
              acquire training and research experience in the field of Science and Technology
              Studies.  The specific research project is a case study of the demand, design,
              manufacture and use of very large mirror blanks in contemporary telescope
              construction.  The study will contribute to understanding of how instruments
              are shaped by technical and scientific considerations and, in turn, shape the
              science that is later done with them.  The study is strongly empirical,
              grounded in detailed observation of scientific and technical work, accompanied
              by reading of the literature, interviews, and examination of the mirrors
              themselves.  The research site is the Steward Observatory Mirror Laboratory at
              the University of Arizona.
