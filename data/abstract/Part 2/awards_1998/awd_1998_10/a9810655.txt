Abstract    :
              This workshop will bring together geographers and scholars from other
              disciplines to discuss issues of race and geography in the United States.  The
              workshop has four objectives: (1) to identify key topics in need of further
              study and to reinvigorate research activity in these areas; (2) to cultivate
              cross-disciplinary research on race relations that involves specialists from
              many fields and backgrounds; (3) to publish a comprehensive report that sets
              the agenda for future research; and (4) to increase minority presence and
              status in the discipline.  The overall intent is to promote a broader and
              deeper geographical appreciation of American race relations and inequalities.
