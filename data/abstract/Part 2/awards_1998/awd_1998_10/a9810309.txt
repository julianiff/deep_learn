Abstract    :
              This award is made under the high performance connections portion of
              ANIR's
"Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 96-64.  It provides
              partial
support for two years for a DS-3 connection to the vBNS. 
              Applications
include projects in high performance scientific computations,
              high energy
physics, software verification, digital libraries, distributed
              simulation
modeling and collaborative circuit design.  Collaborating
              institutions
include the University of Illinois, University of Kentucky,
              Sandia Labs,
Michigan State, California Institute of Technology, Pacific
              Northwest
National Laboratory, Fermi Labs, University of Cinncinati and
              Columbia
University.
