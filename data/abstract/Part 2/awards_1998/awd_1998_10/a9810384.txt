Abstract    :
              A conference on `Integrating GIS and Environmental Modeling: Problems,
              Prospects, and Needs for Research` will be held in May, 1999.  Its purpose is
              to provide a scientific and technical forum for improving spatio-temporal
              environmental modeling.  The outcome of the conference will be the enhancement
              of predictive methods and techniques including process, simulation, stochastic,
              numerical, and other quantitative models.  The overall goal is to integrate GIS
              with these other useful technologies to achieve sound environmental problem
              solving outcomes. The award is directed entirely to support costs associated
              with graduate student travel and dissemination of results.
