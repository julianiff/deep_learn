Abstract    :
              ABSTRACT  "FAMU Connection to vBNS via Florida Gigapop"  Florida A&M University 
              PI: Sterlin Adams  ANI-9810621        This award is made under the high
              performance connections portion of  ANIR's "Connections to the Internet"
              announcement, NSF 96-64.  It provides partial support for two years for an OC-3
              connection to the vBNS.  Applications include projects in interactive
              simulation environments, distributed database processing, computational fluid
              dynamics, distributed real-time computing, and multi-media language interfaces.
               Collaborating institutions include the University of Central Florida,
              University of Houston-Downtown, Grambling State, University of Minnesota and
              Florida International University.
