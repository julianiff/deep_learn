Abstract    :
              This award is made under the high performance connections portion of ANIR's 
              "Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 98-102 (replaces NSF  96-64).
              It  provides for partial support for an OC-3c connection to the very high 
              performance Backbone Network Service (vBNS) for Emory University for two 
              years.  Applications include a "Collaborative Molecular Visualization, Design
              and  Analysis", distance education, real-time collaborative efforts, and remote
               examination of crystallographic data in real time.
