Abstract    :
              ABSTRACT  "vBNS Connectivity for Kent State University"  Kent State University  
              PI: Bruce Petryshak  ANI-9810391        This award is made under the high
              performance connections portion of  ANIR's "Connections to the Internet"
              announcement, NSF 96-64.  It provides partial support for two years for a DS-3
              connection to the vBNS.  Applications include projects in large scalable
              parallel quantum Monte Carlo, liquid crystal research, distributed operating
              systems for high-speed networks, distributed computing, and several mathematics
              projects.  Collaborating institutions include Arizona State, New York
              University, Courant Institute, Case Western Reserve, NIST, University of
              Virginia, University of California San Francisco, Georgetown, Stanford and
              Texas Tech.
