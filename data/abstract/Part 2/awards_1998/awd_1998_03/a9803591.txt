Abstract    :
              This project will complete an interactive multimedia CD-ROM on computer ethics. 
              It will enhance the CD-ROM by adding a specific section on moral decision
              making for computer science and engineering students.  It will re-shoot dated
              video footage of founders of the field of computer ethics, to bring the
              interviews up to date.  Finally, it will create a test instrument to evaluate
              the efficacy of the CD-ROM.
