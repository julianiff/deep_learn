Abstract    :
              An equipment award will support the acquisition of advanced acoustic emission
              and video imaging equipment to be used to study damage development in thermal
              barrier coatings.  The equipment will permit in-situ observation of microscale
              damage and deformation during controlled high temperature experiments.  The
              results will be used to validate an existing model.  The equipment will also be
              used in studies of low temperature plasticity and of damage mechanisms in metal
              matrix composites, and for general non-contact strain measurement in research
              and laboratory classes.
