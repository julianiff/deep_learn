Abstract    :
              This is a Small Grant for Exploratory Research (SGER). The purpose of the
              project is to provide an empirical assessment of the value of immersive virtual
              environments as a methodological tool in social psychology. Virtual environment
              technology has advanced to the point where freely-mobile individuals can be
              placed into contexts that simulate physical and social environments. While this
              technology has broad commercial relevance, it also has important potential for
              the social and behavioral sciences. This potential has not received much
              scientific or scholarly study, which is the purpose of this exploratory
              investigation.
