              Abstract  CTS-9809166  T. G. Theofaneous, UC, Santa Barbara    Partial travel
              support is provided for the participants of the First Annual Meeting of the
              Institute of Multifluid Science and Technology (IMuST) in Santa Barbara,
              February 28 - March 1, 1998.  IMuST promotes critical evaluation of current
              research, interdisciplinary activities, and synergism between fundamental
              research and industrial needs.  This annual meeting will have a focus on
              dispersed systems.  The meeting has three parts, including emerging areas of
              research, technical challenges and education, and an open forum.  The NSF, U.S.
              Department of Energy, U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission, and National
              Aeronautics and Space Administration co-sponsor this meeting.  Approximately 40
              participants will receive partial support.  The Proceedings and other materials
              prepared by the IMuST technical advisory committees will be made available to
              the scientific community and industry.  ***
