Abstract    :
                 This grant awards travel funds to subsidize the airfare for 6 named U.S.
              researchers to participate in the International Workshop on Models of
              Underspecification and the Representation of Meaning, to be held in Bad
              Teinach, Germany, May 18-22, 1998.   The workshop is important to furthering
              the development of linguistic theory, and also applications of linguistics in
              the development of natural language processing software systems, a field in
              which European researchers are particularly active at present.  The grant will
              ensure full U.S. participation in the workshop, and encourage collaborations
              between U.S. and European researchers working in these fields.
