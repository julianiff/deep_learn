This award to University of Texas at Austin will provide
              equipment in support of oceanographic research on R/V Longhorn, a research
              vessel operated by the university's Marine Science Center as part of the
              University-National Oceanographic Laboratory System research fleet. Specific
              equipment to be acquired includes a life raft and an inflatable boat.   The
              shared-use equipment supported here will improve vessel safety and assist
              marine scientists conduct studies from R/V Longhorn, particularly in  the Gulf
              of Mexico, during 1999 and future years.
