Abstract    :
              This award is made under the high performance connections portion of ANIR's
              "Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 98-102.   It provides
              authorization for George Mason University to access the vBNS network as a vBNS
              Authorized Institution (vAI).   Applications include distributed simulation,
              data mining, collaborative Virtual Reality, and distance learning.  
              Collaborating institutions include the Naval Postgraduate School, University of
              Central Florida, and satellite campuses within the George Mason University
              network.
