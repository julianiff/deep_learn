Abstract    :
              ESI-9814003  Horack    This planning grant, which is part of a joint National
              Science Foundation and U.S. Department of Education K-8 mathematics initiative,
              will focus on developing a plan to improve the teaching of mathematics in
              grades K-12 students in the Tucson Unified School District through a
              collaborative effort by resource personnel, teachers administrators, parents,
              community/business members, special populations experts, and the University of
              Arizona.  To provide ongoing district wide professional development & community
              support for reform-based mathematics, an eighty member Teacher Cadre is train
              to both improve the mathematical pedagogy of mathematics teachers and foster
              community support.
