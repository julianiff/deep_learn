Abstract    :
              Flynn  9800505    The Education Development Center (EDC) proposes an inaugural
              conference on behalf of the National Alliance of Presidential Awardees
              Associations (NAPAA).  The conference will set an agenda for the organizations'
              active role in mathematics and science education reform.  The conference will
              involve Awardee representatives from every regional of the country and support
              development of a handbook of guidelines for Presidentail Awardees' work as
              advocates and mentors.  The conference will give NAPAA national visibility and
              promote strategies for using Awardees as resources for reform.
