Abstract    :
              A multimedia software package that exposes undergraduate  students to frontline
              issues in materials research is  developed. The presentation is interactive and
              build on  fundamental concepts to form a logical sequence of steps  that lead
              to the development of a hypothesis or the  discovery of new materials. Several
              `games` and learning  activities are included to stimulate the student's
              interest  and curiosity. The materials issues addressed are those that  are the
              subject of significant recent research activity and  that illustrate the
              interdisciplinary nature of modern  materials science.
