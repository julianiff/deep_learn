Abstract    :
              Profs. Hu and Jacobson are carrying out a broad program of  research in
              theoretical gravitation.  They will apply  techniques from field theory to
              problems in cosmology, black  holes, relativistic thermodynamics, and quantum
              gravity.  Results of their work may lead to important insights, not  only in
              the areas mentioned above, but also in probing the  deeper structures of
              physical laws under extreme conditions  and understanding their manifestations
              in the origin and  dynamics of the universe.
