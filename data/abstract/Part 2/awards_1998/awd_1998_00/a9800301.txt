Abstract    :
              A GOALI award supports studies of the microstructure,  mechanical properties,
              and tribological behavior of thermal  spray functionally graded coatings.  The
              university team  will study the fundamental behavior of the coatings and 
              coordinate with the two industrial partners who will prepare  the coatings and
              test them under bench and field conditions.  Two families of coatings will be
              studied - hard wear  resistant WC-Co coatings produced by Caterpillar, and 
              abradable MCrAlY coatings for high temperature sealing  applications produced
              by Solar Turbines.
