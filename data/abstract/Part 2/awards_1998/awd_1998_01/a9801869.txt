Abstract    :
                   There are two components to this RUI proposal.  The  first continues
              investigations of Rydberg atoms, focusing on  the structure and evolution of
              Rydberg wavepackets in  external fields. The second project develops a new 
              theoretical framework for examining CPT violation on  particles and
              antiparticles in Penning traps.
