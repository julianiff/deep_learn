Abstract    :
              Anna Secor  University of Colorado, Boulder  The doctoral dissertation research
              proposal will provide a case study of Islamist politics and women's roles in an
              urban Turkish context, Istanbul.  The research will construct an urban
              geography of Istanbul, explore the bases of support for Islamist politics, and
              investigate women's role in these activities. Census data and personal
              interviews will be conducted and analyzed using qualitative and quantitative
              methods in a three-phase structure. The results will provide new knowledge on
              women's roles in politics and will provide new insights into Islam at the nexus
              of global forces and local cultures.
