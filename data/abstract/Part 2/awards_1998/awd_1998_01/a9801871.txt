Abstract    :
                   This project will examine three problems.  The first is  that of inelastic
              collisions of highly excited Rydberg atoms  with ground state atoms resulting
              in electron transfer to  the neutral to form a negative ion.  The second is 
              vibrational excitation and dissociative attachment of slow  electrons to
              molecules.  The third will be to generalize the  theory of dissociative
              attachment to molecules physisorbed  on surfaces and embedded in crystal media.
