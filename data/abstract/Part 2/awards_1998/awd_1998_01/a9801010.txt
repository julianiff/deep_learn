Abstract    :
              Clarke-MXR will develop a compact THz  time domain spectrometer.  Data collected
              using this instrument can be used to reconstruct the absorption spectrum of any
              non-metallic material through which THz radiation has passed.  Applications for
              this spectrometer include : semiconductor wafer analysis; gas constituent
              analysis, combustion process characterization, and probing of pre-formed
              plastic.
