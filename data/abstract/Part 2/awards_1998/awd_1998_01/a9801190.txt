Abstract    :
              On-Line Technologies, Inc. will develop technology enabling wafer-by-wafer
              closed loop control of semiconductor process equipment.  A closed-loop cell
              control system providing robust, operator-free, automated, closed-loop control 
              will be developed, which can improve yield, reduce the need for test wafers,
              decrease operating and testing manpower, shorten development cycles and reduce
              downtime in the semiconductor industry.  These improvements could increase the
              capacity of a fabrication line by up to 30%.
