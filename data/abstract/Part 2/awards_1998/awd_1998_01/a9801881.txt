Abstract    :
                   The Moriond series of conferences cover the status of  particle physics. 
              The first focuses on the electroweak  theory of particle physics and the second
              on Quantum  Chromodynamics.  The conferences are attended by scientists  from
              all over the world and this grant will enable several  students to participate
              in the meetings.
