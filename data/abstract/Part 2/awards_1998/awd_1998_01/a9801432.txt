Abstract    :
              Uniax Corp. will develop large size, linear photodiode arrays and 2D photodiode
              matrices fabricated from semiconducting polymers and polymer blends.  The
              proposed innovation will have impact in the digital camera market by enabling a
              much lower cost sensor head.  It is also expected to  have impact in digital
              scanner heads and a variety of sensor heads for scientific and commercial
              applications.
