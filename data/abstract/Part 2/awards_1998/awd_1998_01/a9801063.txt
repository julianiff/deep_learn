Abstract    :
              WaveBand Corp. will develop a low-cost millimeter wave radiometric imaging
              system, using a single, high quality millimeter wave detector with a rapidly
              scanning low-cost antenna.  Millimeter wave imaging systems have many important
              applications, including aircraft navigation, fire detection, contraband
              detection, automobile collision avoidance, ice mapping and remote sensing of
              the earth's resources.
