              Schweickert  The Jurassic volcanic arc has been shown to have a complex history
              and may have involved multiple episodes of extensional, contractional and
              strike-slip deformation.  This project will attempt to reveal the geometry and
              style of Mesozoic interarc detachment faults and their relation to igneous
              activity and to preceding and ensuring contractional events.  The time-space
              relationships of deformation and plutonism need to be resolved so that they can
              be related to plate boundary processes and to back-arc phenomena.
