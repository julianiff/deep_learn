Abstract    :
              9529273  Crotts    Dr. Crotts will continue a pilot survey of gravitational 
              microlensing of stars in the galaxy M31 with the intent of  capitalizing on the
              unique capabilities of this kind of study in  ways that are complementary to
              microlensing surveys for MACHOs in  the directions of the Large Magellanic
              Cloud and the bulge of our  Galaxy.  M31 may be the only external spiral galaxy
              that can be  successfully probed for dark matter with the microlensing 
              technique.  ***
