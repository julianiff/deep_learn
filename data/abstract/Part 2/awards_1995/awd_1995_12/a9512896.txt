Abstract    :
                This three-year award supports U.S.-France cooperative research in condensed
              matter physics between Angus Kingon at North Carolina State University and
              Bernard Agius at the University of Paris, Orsay.  The objective of their
              research is to study the use of ferroelectric thin films in advanced
              technologies.  They will investigate materials integration of thin films
              processing with regard to use in thin-film devices, and the impact of
              microstructure on fundamental properties.  This project brings together
              complementary U.S. and French expertise in thin film growth and
              characterization. Ferroelectric films have potential applications in capacitor
              materials, filters, pressure sensors and other devices.
