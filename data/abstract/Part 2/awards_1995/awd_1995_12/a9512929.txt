Abstract    :
                This three-year award supports U.S.-France cooperative research in biophysics
              between Stephen Slatin, Einstein College of Medicine and Daniel Baty at the
              Laboratory of Engineering and Dynamics of Membrane Systems, French National
              Center for Scientific Research, Marseille.  The objective of their research is
              to study at the molecular level, the mechanisms by which ion channels are gated
              by voltage, and the mechanisms that govern protein/lipid interactions.  These
              processes are crucial to the functioning of most cells.  The investigators will
              study the colicin crystal structure.  The project will benefit from
              complementary U.S. and French expertise and by combining the different
              techniques and protein studies of the two groups.
