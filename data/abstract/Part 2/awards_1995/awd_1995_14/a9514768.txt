Abstract    :
                   This program supports the activities of the Plasma  Science Committee of
              the National Research Council.  This  committee is the standing committee of
              the NRC charged with  monitoring the health of the field of plasma science in
              the  United States.  It does this through regular meetings,  panels, and
              workshops, with participation by active  researchers in the plasma science
              community.  It  disseminates the results of its deliberations through  reports
              and through direct conversations with community  members and with
              representatives of funding agencies.
