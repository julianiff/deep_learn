              9514174  Scheppele  This Small Grant for Exploratory Research (SGER)  will
              support the P.I.'s observation of the continuing development of the democratic
              process in Hungary.  It will make it possible for her to sit in as an observer
              and expert during the sessions of the Hungarian Parliamentary Drafting
              Committee that will be producing a new constitution during 1995-96.  It will
              advance materially her research on how constitutional thinking emerges by
