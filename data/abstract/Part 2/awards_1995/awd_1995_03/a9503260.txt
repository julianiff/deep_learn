Abstract    :
              The focus of this research is on natural product synthesis and relies on   the
              development of biomimetic cyclizations, glycosylations and enzymatic  
              transformations.  Target molecules include the cycloviracins, phenoxan   and
              hennoxazole, all of which have antiviral properties.  The focus of   the
              educational plan involves strategies for the recruitment of Hispanic   students
              into the field of chemistry.  With this CAREER award, the Synthetic Organic
              Program is supporting the   research and educational plan of Dr. Michael R.
              Pena of the Department of   Chemistry at Arizona State University.  Professor
              Pena will focus his   research efforts on the development of new synthetic
              pathways for the   preparation of four natural products, all of which have
              interesting   biological properties.  The focus of Dr. Pena's educational plan
              involves   strategies for the recruitment  of Hispanic students into the field
              of chemistry.
