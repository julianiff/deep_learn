Abstract    :
                9503922  Beall    This project connects St. John's College to the Internet
              over a 56 kbps  line.  Faculty and students at the school benefit from access
              to the  resources of the Internet, including libraries and supercomputers.  In 
              addition, they are able to communicate and collaborate with colleagues at 
              other institutions in pursuit of research and educational projects.  The  award
              provides partial support of the project for one year.
