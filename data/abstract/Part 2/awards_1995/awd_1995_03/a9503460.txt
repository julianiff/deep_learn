Abstract    :
              9503460  Abraham      This project connects Siena Heights College to the
              Internet over a  T1 (1.544 thousand bit per second) line through MichNet. 
              Faculty  and students will benefit from access to the resources of the 
              Internet, including libraries and supercomputers.  In addition,  they will be
              able to communicate and collaborate with colleagues at  other institutions in
              pursuit of research and educational projects.    The award provides partial
              support of the project for two years.
