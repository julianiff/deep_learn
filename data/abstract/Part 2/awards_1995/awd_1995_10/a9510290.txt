              9510290  Hayes    This project connects the Telemedicine Research Center  to the
              Internet over a 56 kbps line.  Faculty and students at the school benefit from
              access to the resources of the Internet, including libraries and
              supercomputers.  In addition, they are able to communicate and collaborate with
              colleagues at other institutions in pursuit of research and educational
              projects.  The award provides partial support of the project for one year.
