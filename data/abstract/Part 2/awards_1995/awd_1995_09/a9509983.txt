Abstract    :
              9509983  Miller  This project organizes and presents an intensive 
              multidisciplinary survey conference on the implications for  privacy and
              freedom of interaction of computers, computer  databanks, and computer
              networks.  This conference will bring  together individuals from academia,
              government and industry to  focus on information technology issues in the area
              of  legislation, regulation, security, law enforcement, civil  liberties,
              freedom and privacy.  The award provides partial  support for speaker travel
              expenses, publication of materials and  audio visual equipment rental for this
              conference.
