Abstract    :
              9521766  Scruggs  The University of Southern Mississippi requests support from
              NSF  for a connection to the Internet through the MISNET regional  network. 
              MISNET (MISsissippi Higher Education Research NETwork) is  the midlevel network
              located in the state of Mississippi that will  provide operations and network
              information services. It will  provide the University of Southern Mississippi
              with a connection to  the Internet, a collection of interoperable networks that
              connect  most research and education organizations in the United States  The 
              university needs the Internet connection to be able to access  information
              resources throughout the state, the nation and the  world. The connection will
              benefit the faculty, staff students of  the University of Southern Mississippi.
