              9521790  Schwartz    This project connects the Institute of American Indian Arts
              to the Internet over a 56Kbps line.  Faculty and students at the Institute
              benefit from access to the resources of the Internet, including libraries and
              art collections.  In addition, they are able to communicate and collaborate
              with colleagues at other institutions in pursuit of artistic and educational
              projects.  The award provides partial support of the project for two years.
