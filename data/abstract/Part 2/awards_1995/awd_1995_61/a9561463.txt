Abstract    :
                        This Small Business Innovation Research Phase I project further
              develops the use of Pr:YLF as a unique lasing crystal specifically for pulsed
              laser holography. In this project a method of pumping Pr:YLF with pulses of
              blue laser light derived from the frequency doubled output of a Cr:LiSAF laser
              is demonstrated. Outputs in red, green and blue will permit true color pulsed
              laser holography for the first time.   Medical imaging, particularly for
              diagnosis of the human eye, as well as display holography and replacement of
              helium cadmium lasers are just a few of the applications of this technology.
