Abstract    :
               This meeting was addressed by invited speakers who have   made outstanding
              contributions to  the field of  computer algebra.  The speakers anchored the 
              meeting and    helped attract  participants from the computer  algebra or
              computational   scientific/engineering  communities from  the eastern half of
              the US.  The invited   talks and poster  sessions contributed by the community 
              covered the breadth of    algebra: algorithms, software, and applications.  The
                 principle theme of this  l995 meeting was applications.    The purpose of
              this meeting   was to stimulate  interest in and to  enhance understanding of
              the technical   aspects of computer  algebra, by providing a way for those in
              the region who are    active or  interested in becoming more active in computer
              algebra  to   meet with each other  in an inexpensive and easily accessible 
              way.
