Abstract    :
                  A Workshop on Computer Support for Policy Analysis and  Design was supported
              by this award.  This is a topic that has been  addressed in many  journal and
              conference papers, but never  in a coordinated way: thus a paper on  security
              policy seldom  deals with safety or high-speed response considerations,  except
              as a footnote or ending paragraph.  The purpose of  this workshop was to 
              provide a forum in which researchers in different fields  could discuss their
              interests in computer support  for  policy.
