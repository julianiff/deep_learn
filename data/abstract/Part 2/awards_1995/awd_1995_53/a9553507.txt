              9553507  Granger    The University of Missouri, in collaboration wilth St. Louis

              Missouri.  The program provides a  research experience with a university

              physics, and  psychology.  In addition to a research experience and clasroom 
              lectures and discussion in mathematics and various scientific  disciplines,
              students are provided with formal instruciton on  technical communication
              skills and career confabs which expose them  to a variety of technically
              oriented job possibilities.
