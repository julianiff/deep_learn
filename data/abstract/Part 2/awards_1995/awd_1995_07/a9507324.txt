              9507324  Stillman   The meeting, "Eukaryotic DNA Replication" will be held at
              Cold Spring Harbor , September 6-10, 1995. This conference is held every two
              years and is devoted to topics related to chromosome duplication, structure and
              function and will include active areas of research in cell growth and cell
              cycle control, DNA amplification, virology and developmental biology. Senior
              scientists, post-doctoral fellows and graduate students will meet in an
              environment conductive and encouraging to intense communication.   ***
