Abstract    :
              Dr. Lon Knight and other members of the Chemistry Department at  Furman  
              University are being supported to continue a Research Experiences for  
              Undergraduates (REU) site in chemistry.  For the period 1996-98, ten  
              undergraduate students will spend ten weeks each summer actively engaged   in
              basic research projects drawn from the traditional areas of chemistry   and
              biochemistry. The REU students are joined by 20 additional   undergraduate
              researchers and visiting faculty members from regional   colleges to form a
              vibrant research community with a strong record of   co-publication.
