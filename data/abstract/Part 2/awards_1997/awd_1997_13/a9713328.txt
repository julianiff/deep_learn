Abstract    :
              The purpose of the workshop is to bring together experts for  intensive
              discussions of progress and prospects in the  technologies of polarized ion and
              electron beams and  polarized gas targets.  Measurement of spin observables at 
              IUCF, TJNAF, Bates, and HERMES is a high priority for  nuclear physics as
              outlined in the NSAC Long Range Plan.
