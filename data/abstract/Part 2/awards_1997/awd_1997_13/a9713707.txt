Abstract    :
                   This grant funds research on geometric metrology.  Geometric metrology is
              an approach to the dimensioning and tolerancing of mechanical parts.  The goal
              of this research is to extend the methodology of geometric metrology to a
              larger class of applications, develop the theoretical structure of geometric
              metrology, incorporate scientific visualization elements within a unified
              geometric metrology toolbox, and explore areas within metrology that can
              benefit from this research.       If successful, this research will contribute
              to the ability of designers to specify geometric tolerances on parts that will
              lead to improved quality control and lower product cost.
