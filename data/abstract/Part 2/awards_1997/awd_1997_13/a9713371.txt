Abstract    :
              The PI will establish a new research program investigating the metallisation 
              schemes which offer potential for implementation if wide band-gap (GaN, SiC)
              semiconductor devices.  Her research will combine knowledge of interfacial
              reactions  and materials properties with an understanding of the physics of
              Schottky barriers and the associated interfacial current transport mechanisms.
