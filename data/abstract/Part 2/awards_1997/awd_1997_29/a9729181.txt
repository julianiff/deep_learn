              9729181  Bacon    The principal investigator and his colleagues at the Woods

              Joint Global Ocean Flux Study (U.S.  JGOFS). This will include the planning of

              U.S. JGOFS process studies and the  continuation of the time-series stations at
              Hawaii (HOT) and  Bermuda (BATS).
