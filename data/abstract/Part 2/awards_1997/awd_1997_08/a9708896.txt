Abstract    :
              This award from the Chemistry Research Instrumentation and Facilities   Program
              (CRIF) will assist the junior faculty in Department of Chemistry   at the
              University of Missouri at Columbia to acquire a femtosecond laser   system.
              This equipment will enhance research into solvation and   reactivity in
              supercritical fluid solutions.   A femtosecond laser provides ultrafast pulses
              of coherent visible  or   infrared light which allows researchers to obtain
              important information   about fast occurring chemical reactions. Its use may
              enable breakthroughs   in our understanding of the properties of reactive and
              nonreactive   molecules
