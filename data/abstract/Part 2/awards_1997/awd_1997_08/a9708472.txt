Abstract    :
              Charles Knobler of the Chemistry Department of U of California-Los   Angeles  is
              funded by the Physical Chemistry Program of the Chemistry   Division to study
              the equilibrium and flow properties of monolayers. In   this research
              experiments in five related areas will be conducted: (1)   Flow properties of
              Langmuir monolayers, (2) phase transitions in   monolayers, (3) textures and
              domain shapes of Langmuir-Blodgett films,   (4) the mechanism of Langmuir
              monolayer collapse and (5) the adsorption   on patterned films.  This research
              has wide implications for chemistry and biology.  The   Langmuir-Blodgett films
              find application in surface modification to   enhance or inhibit adsorption in
              the development of biochemical sensors
