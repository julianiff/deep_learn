Abstract    :
              A conference will be held on the subject of quantum  chromodynamics in nuclear
              physics, part of the Gordon  Research Conference series.  The outcome of this
              meeting  will be the dissemination of the latest experimental results  and
              theoretical thinking on this subject, which is among the  most important in the
              field of nuclear physics.  The NSF  funds will support young faculty, postdocs,
              and graduate  students working in this high priority field, as established  by
              the joint NSF/DOE Nuclear Science Advisory Committee in  its 1996 Long Range
              Plan.  Education of students and  postdocs is an important component of this
              award.
