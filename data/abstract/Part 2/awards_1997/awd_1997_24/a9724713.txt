Abstract    :
                   Line broadening plays an important role in high-  pressure, industrially
              important lighting plasmas.  In this  GOALI project a joint
              experimental/theoretical study of line  broadening in sodium related plasmas
              will be conducted  between the Smithsonian Astrophysical Observatory and OSRAM 
              Sylvania Inc.  These studies not only impact our  understanding of commercially
              important lighting sources but  are also needed to model other plasma processes
              where  radiative transport is important.  This project is jointly  supported by
              the Office of Multidisciplinary Activities of  the Directorate for Mathematical
              and Physical Sciences and  the Division of Physics at the NSF.
