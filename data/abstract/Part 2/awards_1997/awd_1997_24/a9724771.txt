Abstract    :
                   This grant will provide support for junior researchers  to attend a
              three-day workshop whose focus is the physics of  excited baryons.  This topic
              is timely in light of a program  of experiments which are now underway at the
              Thomas  Jefferson National Accelerator Facility, and whose purpose  is to
              provide a better understanding of the underlying  structure of protons,
              neutrons and their excited states.  Speakers at the workshop are experts in the
              subtopics which  will be covered by the workshop.
