Abstract    :
              This proposal requests support for event trigger development  and electronics
              acquisition for the D0 experiment at  Fermilab.  This project will allow the
              detector to be  effective when exposed, the higher beam intensities are 
              participating in this effort.  New real time triggers to  handle higher rates
              are essential, and will involve novel  combinations of serial and parallel
              processors to do the  job.
