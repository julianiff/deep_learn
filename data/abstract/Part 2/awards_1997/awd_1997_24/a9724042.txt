Abstract    :
                This award supports a planning visit by Dr. Altug Iftar, of  the Department of
              Electrical and Electronics Engineering at  Anadolu University in Turkey, to
              visit Ohio State University  to meet with Dr. Hitay Ozbay, of the Department of
               Electrical Engineering.  The purpose of the visit is to  develop a
              collaborative research activity on an integrated  routing and flow control
              algorithm for traffic networks.  Little research has been done on the
              integration of routing  and flow control algorithms, and the results of this
              study  are expected to yield useful results that can be applied to  traffic
              control, communications, and manufacturing systems.
