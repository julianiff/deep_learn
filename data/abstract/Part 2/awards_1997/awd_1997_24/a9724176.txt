Abstract    :
                   The BLANCA experiment will use an array of detectors to  collect the light
              emitted by cosmic ray showers.  Analysis  of the characteristics of this light
              will allow the energy  of the shower to be measured and an estimate to be made
              of  how far above the surface of the earth the shower  originated.  This
              information will enable experimenters to  determine what type of particles
              caused the shower, which  information in turn will provide clues as to the
              origin of  cosmic rays.
