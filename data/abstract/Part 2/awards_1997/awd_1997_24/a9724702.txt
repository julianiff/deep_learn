Abstract    :
                   This grant is for support of junior attendees to the  1997 Gordon Research
              Conference on Atomic Physics, to be  held in New Hampshire, June 28-July 4,
              1997.  This  conference is a major source of communication of new  research
              directions in atomic, molecular and optical physics  and support of the meeting
              enables students and postdocs to  contribute to and be exposed to these new
              areas
