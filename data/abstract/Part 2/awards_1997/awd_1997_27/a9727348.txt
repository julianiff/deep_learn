Abstract    :
                Funds are provided to examine sediment transport processes in three estuaries
              in South Carolina or Georgia.  Each study will focus on the physical processes
              responsible for trapping sediments within the estuary.  Support is also
              provided for Project Interface, which is a goal-oriented, multi-year program
              for undergraduates to attain the skills of scientific thinking, methods, and
              communication.  The study of sediment dynamics in Winyah Bay will provide the
              platform for multi-disciplinary studies of productivity, nutrient and organic
              carbon cycling, and physical-chemical estuarine processes.
