Abstract    :
              The focus of this research is development of organosilyl triflates as  
              precursors for intramolecular Diels-Alder reactions and intramolecular   ene
              reactions.  The potential for the methodology will be illustrated  by   a
              synthesis of reserpine.  With this new award, the Synthetic Organic Program and
              the Office of   Multidisciplinary Activities are supporting the research of Dr.
              Scott M.   Sieburth of the Department of Chemistry at the State University of
              New   York, Stony Brook.  Professor Sieburth will focus his work on developing 
               organosilicon methodology which can be used for the synthesis of natural  
              products that have interesting biological properties.
