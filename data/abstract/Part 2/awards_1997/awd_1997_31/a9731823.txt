Abstract    :
              During the next three summer, the Chemistry Division will support a   Research
              Experience for Undergraduates (REU) site in the Chemistry   Department,
              Michigan State University, coordinated by Dr. Michael W.   Rathke.  During the
              10-week experience, ten students will investigate the   synthesis, modeling and
              characterization of materials.  The program will   be augmented by weekly
              seminars on topics in materials and in careers in   chemistry and by field
              trips to Dow Chemical and Ford Motor Company.  At   the end of the summer,
              students will give oral presentations on their   results, as well as
              participate in a regional poster session for all the   chemistry REU sites in
              the Michigan-Ohio area.
