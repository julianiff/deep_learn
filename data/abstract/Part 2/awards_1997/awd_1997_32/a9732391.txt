Abstract    :
              This Chemistry Division award provides support for a Research Experiences   for
              Undergraduates (REU) site in the Chemistry Department, University of   Puerto
              Rico, Rio Piedras, directed by Dr. Ana R. Guadalupe.  Ten students   will
              participate each year over the next three years in an 8-week summer   program. 
              The students will be exposed to a variety of research topics   across all the
              fields of chemistry, and through a series of orientation   lectures, seminars,
              and workshops they will be introduced to safety   practices, ethical issues,
              modern chemical instrumentation, communication   and presentation skills, and
              use of the internet.  Students will be   required to report research results at
              an undergraduate research symposium
