Abstract    :
               Computer systems of the future may consist of thousands or even millions   of
              processors.  Inevitably some of the processors will  fail.  Faulty  processors
              need to be repaired so that the system  on continue functioning.  The goal of
              this project is to investigate  improvements to the existing   algorithms for
              parallel fault  diagnosis.  Second, and more importantly,   the project will
              develop dynamic models for fault diagnosis that are more    realistic than 
              previous models.
