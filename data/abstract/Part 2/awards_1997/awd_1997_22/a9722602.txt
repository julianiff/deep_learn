Abstract    :
              This is a program to investigate ultra-cold atoms near the  ionization
              threshold.  Rubidium atoms will be chilled using  laser techniques, followed by
              excitation just below the  ionization limit to produce a gas of ultra-cold
              Rydberg  atoms or ionization just above the threshold to produce an  ultra-cold
              plasma.  Once these states are reached, atomic  interactions and plasma physics
              will be explored in this  entirely new parameter space of extremely low kinetic
              and  potential energies.  Collective effects are expected to  emerge for the
              Rydberg species, while the ionized state  should demonstrate properties of a
              strongly-coupled neutral  plasma.
