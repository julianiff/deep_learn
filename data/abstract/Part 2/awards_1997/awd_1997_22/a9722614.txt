Abstract    :
              This research project in the general area of quantum  interferometry will
              investigate interference phenomena at  both the single and multiparticle
              levels.  A varied set of  experiments will be conducted, all of which have as
              their  focus some property of entangled states or quantum  measurement and
              communication.  Atoms, neutrons, and photons  will be used as the interfering
              elements.  The experimental  component will be accompanied by theoretical
              development in  the general area of quantum interference.
