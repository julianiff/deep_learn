Abstract    :
              This action will extend the work in progress work by a group  at the University
              of Colorado to develop advanced  instrumentation for the Laser Interferometer
              Gravitational  Wave Observatory (LIGO).  As part of a collaborative effort 
              they will pursue numerical modeling to continue development  of an active
              seismic isolation system for LIGO.  The  modeling techniques they develop will
              be used by other  groups working on isolation systems related to LIGO.  This 
              project is jointly supported by the Division of Physics and  the MPS Office of
              Multidisciplinary Activities.
