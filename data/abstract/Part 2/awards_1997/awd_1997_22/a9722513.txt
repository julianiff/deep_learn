Abstract    :
                   Research is proposed that has the potential to enhance  the performance of
              accelerators.  The topics are related to  the interactions of accelerator beams
              in colliders,  optimizations of storage rings, beam size growth and  particle
              loss, and improvements in beam-dynamics  calculations.
