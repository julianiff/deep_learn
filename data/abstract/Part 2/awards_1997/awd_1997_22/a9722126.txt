Abstract    :
                  This grant will fund studies of the performance of a  proposed space
              telescope which if built will study high  energy gamma rays.  The results of
              the studies will help  ensure that the telescope will be able to achieve its
              design  goals.  The work will study specific types of events that  the
              telescope could search for and establish the feasibility  of the proposed
              measurements.  If built, the telescope will  search for such processes as gamma
              ray production from  evaporating black holes.
