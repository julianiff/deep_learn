Abstract    :
              This research will focus on the development of theories and  models capable of
              describing the varied phenomena expected  to influence the propagation of
              ultra-intense, ultra-short  laser pulses through gases.  Particular emphasis
              will be  given to propagation through channels.  Both analytic  theories and
              computational models will be explored, the  latter being derived from the
              simulation code WAKE.  An  extension of the code from the current
              two-dimensional form  to the three-dimensional form will also be undertaken. 
              This  work is supported as part of the NSF/DOE Partnership in  Basic Plasma
              Science and Engineering.
