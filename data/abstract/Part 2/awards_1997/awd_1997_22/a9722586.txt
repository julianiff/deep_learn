Abstract    :
              This research program utilizes multiple highly correlated  photons to study
              features of quantum interference and  entanglement.  The photons are generated
              in precisely timed  parametric down converters.  The availability of quantum 
              sources that have a classical timing signal makes possible  experiments that
              exhibit new effects that can be ascribed to  a single excitation of a quantum
              field.  A number of  different projects are targeted, including measuring the 
              wave function of a single photon and testing conclusions of  local hidden
              variable theories.
