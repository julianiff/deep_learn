Abstract    :
                   The time-dependent Schr`dinger equation for atoms and  quantum dots in
              strong femtosecond laser field is solved  using a novel basis set selection
              technique.  The objective  is to study the effects of electron correlation on
              the  dynamical properties of these systems.  The research is  partially
              supported by the Division of Physics, the Division  of Materials Science and
              Office of Multidisciplinary  Activities of the Mathematics and Physical
              Sciences  Directorate.
