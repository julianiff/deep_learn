Abstract    :
                   Three problems involving the interaction of charged  particles in a
              non-equilibrium plasma are studied using the  theoretical tools of statistical
              mechanics coupled with  numerical simulation.  The primary objective of the
              research  is to qualitatively explain features from recent experiments  and to
              discover the underlying mechanism for the motion of  charged particles in a
              plasma and plasmas under the  influence of external fields.
