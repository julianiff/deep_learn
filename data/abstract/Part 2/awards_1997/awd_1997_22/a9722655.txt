Abstract    :
                   This grant will support the construction and operation  of a detector which
              will study high energy photons or gamma  rays.  Measuring the energy of these
              particles and  determining their origin will help answer many outstanding 
              questions in astrophysics.  This will help us to understand  pulsars and the
              phenomena of gamma rays bursts.  No detector  has yet made measurements of
              photons in the energy range  this detector will probe which means that it is
              possible  that existing new results will be obtained.
