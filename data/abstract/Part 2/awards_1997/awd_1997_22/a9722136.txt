Abstract    :
                   Theoretical calculations of the collisions of electrons  with molecular
              ions are performed using both time-dependent  wavepacket techniques and the
              complex Kohn variational  method.  The goal is to make quantitative predictions
              of the  details of the cross sections for a broad range of molecular 
              collisions.
