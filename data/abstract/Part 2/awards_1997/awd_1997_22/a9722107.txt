Abstract    :
                   This award is for support of the Institute for  Theoretical Atomic and
              Molecular Physics (ITAMP) at the  Harvard/Smithsonian.  The ITAMP is the focal
              point for group  activities in theoretical atomic and molecular physics in  the
              US.  The funds support a long-and -short term visitor  program, postdoctorals,
              graduate students and topical  workshops on various subjects of interest to the
              atomic and  molecular physics community.   ITAMP activities have been 
              instrumental in the revitalization of atomic and molecular  theory in this
              country.
