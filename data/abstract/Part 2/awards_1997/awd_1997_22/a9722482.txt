Abstract    :
                   There are six quarks.  The fifth type of quark-the b  quark-was discovered
              twenty years ago.  This conference  commemorates that discovery by reviewing
              what is known about  this quark and what still has to be learnt.  The grant
              will  subsidize the attendance and participation of younger  scientists at the
              conference.
