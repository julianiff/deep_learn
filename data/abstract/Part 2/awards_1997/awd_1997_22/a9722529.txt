Abstract    :
              Travel funds will be provided for U.S. scientists to attend  the Eighth Marcel
              Grossmann Meeting on Recent Developments  in Theoretical and Experimental
              General Relativity,  Gravitation, and Relativistic Field Theories.  The Marcel 
              Grossmann meeting will take place on June 22-27, l997 in  Jerusalem, Israel. 
              The availability of funds will be  announced through the electronic mail
              distribution list  maintained by Malcolm MacCallum, in the newsletter for the 
              APS Topical Group on Gravitation, and in general conference  announcements. 
              The applications will be reviewed by a panel  of U.S. scientists representing a
              balanced mixture of  research interests and expertise.
