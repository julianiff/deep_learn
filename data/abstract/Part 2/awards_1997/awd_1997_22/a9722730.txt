Abstract    :
                This one-year award will support participation of US students and young
              scientists to   attend the Intelligent Systems in Molecular Biology (ISMB)
              Conference to be held in   Greece , 21-24 June 1997.    This is the fifth in
              this newly established conference in the   subfield of computational biology,
              application of intelligent systems to problems in   molecular biology.  The
              1997 conference is the second of these conferences held at an   international
              site.  Its goals include convening a critical mass of researchers applying  
              advanced computational techniques to problems in molecular biology, creating
              cross-  disciplinary educational opportunities, and fostering the involvement
              of women and   minorities.    This award will support the participation in the
              conference of up to 20 graduate students   or postdoctoral fellows, who are
              co-authors of papers submitted to the conference.  The   European site of the
              meeting will provide international opportunities for US researchers at   an
              early stage in their careers.
