Abstract    :
                   This research focuses on the development of high  precision tracking
              devices.  These will be part of large  scale particle physics detectors
              operating at the highest  machine energies available.  These devices, which use
               silicon as the detecting element, will help physicists  reconstruct the
              details of how particles decay.  This, in  turn, will lead to an increased
              understanding of the physics  processes involved.  The ultimate goal of these
              experiments  is to discover the Higgs particle - the `missing link` of  the
              Standard Model of particle physics.  The technology  needed to make these
              devices work is at the cutting edge.  Its successful development will lead to
              applications in many  other fields of science and industry.  The PI will
              directly  involve undergraduates in this research, preparing them for  careers
              in many high-tech industries.  This award will also  help fund her outreach
              program directed at women in high  schools.
