Abstract    :
              Physical Sciences Inc. will demonstrate a selective anisotropic etching tool for
              silicon dioxide, using a hypothermal Flourine atom beam based upon its patented
              Fast Atom Sampler Test (FAST) technology.  The energy range of the FAST source
              enables highly efficient, damage-free etching of SiO2.  The anisotropic, low
              damage capability targeted is very relevant to the Semiconductor Industry
              Association roadmap, which defines neutral stream processing as a critical
              technology for etching sub 0.2 ªm features in silicon dioxide in the
              fabrication of ULSI devices.
