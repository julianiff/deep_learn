Abstract    :
              Coherent Technologies Inc. will develop compact, efficient, robust solid state
              lasers operating in the near and mid-infared spectral regions, that provide
              multi-watt output power, broad continuous tunability, and room temperature
              operation.  These lasers will be based on chromium and cobalt-doped zinc
              chalcogenide hosts, and will play an essential role in a variety of
              applications including remote sensing, spectroscopy and in medical science in
              both surgical and dermatological use.
