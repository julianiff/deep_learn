Abstract    :
              Professor Kahn and his graduate student will study high performance, free-space
              infrared (IR) wireless communications systems.  The team will seek to improve
              the performance of such systems by enabling the transmission of higher bit
              rates, by increasing transmission ranges,  by improving link reliability, and
              by increasing network capacities.  These improvements will be sought while
              simultaneously investigating the fundamental limits of IR technology in
              wireless communication applications.
