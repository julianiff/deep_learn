Abstract    :
              This award is made under the high performance connections portion of NCRI's 
              "Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 96-64.  It provides partial 
              support for two years for a OC-3 connection to the vBNS.  Applications  include
              projects in physics, astronomy, computer science, speech  processing, earth
              sciences, library technology and distance education.  Collaborating
              institutions include the University of Wisconsin at Madison,  University of
              Chicago, Princeton University, University of Washington,  Fermi National
              Accelerator Laboratory, Pittsburgh Supercomputing Center and  the San Diego
              Supercomputing Center.
