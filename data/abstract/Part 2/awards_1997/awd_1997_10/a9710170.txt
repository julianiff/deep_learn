Abstract    :
              In this Minority Research Planning Grant (MRPG), the PI will develop a research
              program directed at the study of business process reengineering (BPR).  The PI
              notes that the development and implementation of re-engineering programs fall
              into radical and incremental approaches.  Her efforts in this research will be
              two-fold:  (1) to identify and validate the critical factors that can make
              re-engineering a success or failure and (2) to investigate modeling and
              simulation methodology suitable for re-engineering processes.
