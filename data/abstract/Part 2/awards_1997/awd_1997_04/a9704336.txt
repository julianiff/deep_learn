Abstract    :
              The proposed research consists of accelerator based  experimental programs at
              three laboratories -Brookhaven  National Laboratory, Thomas Jefferson National
              Accelerator  Facility, and the European Synchrotron Radiation Facility.  The
              experiments at all three facilities use photonuclear  reactions to investigate
              the production of mesons and  nucleon resonances, as well as fundamental
              properties of the  neutron and proton. A dominant component of each program is 
              the scattering of polarized photons from polarized nucleons.  The experiments
              address fundamental questions in nuclear and  particle physics.
