Abstract    :
                   This NSF CAREER proposal uses the most modern  theoretical and
              computational tools to study time-dependent  phenomena in atomic systems.  The
              proposed research focuses  on 1) the effect of very intense laser fields to
              produce  multiphoton ionization in atoms and 2) the theoretical  treatment of
              ultrafast optical pulses both above and below  the optical limit.  The PI
              intends to use the visualization  techniques developed in his research on
              time-dependent  quantum mechanics to make several areas of classical and 
              quantum physics more attractive and easier to comprehend by  students at both
              the undergraduate and graduate levels.
