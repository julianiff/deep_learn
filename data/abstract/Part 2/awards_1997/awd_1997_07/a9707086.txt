              9707086  GREENE       In 1997 the R/V POINT SUR will provide shiptime to 
              scientist and  students  from the Naval Postgraduate School,  University  of
              California  at Santa Cruz, University of  Southern California,  US Geological 
              Survey, Monterey Bay  Aquarium Research Institute  and Moss  Landing Marine 
              Laboratories, all are members of the Central California  Oceanographic 
              Cooperative  (CENCAL).   In  addition,  researchers  from  Monterey Bay
              Aquarium,  Lamonat-Doherty  Earth Observatory, State  University  of  New 
              York,  University  of  Washington  Naval  Oceanographic Office and the 
              National  Marine Fisheries will use the POINT SUR.        The POINT SUR is
              scheduled for 197 operational days in  1997 of which 82 are in support of NSF
              programs.  The vessel  will work mainly off the coast of California with one
              cruise  off the  coast of Mexico.  ***
