Abstract    :
              9700827  Coleman    The NSF/Engineering Education Coalition, SUCCEED, proposes
              to host a conference for the various participants in the listed Engineering
              Education Programs.  The conference is planned for the Spring of 1997 in
              Washington, DC.  The conference will be preceded by a planning session
              involving representatives of the various programs.  The conference will employ
              formal TQ tools to produce a "best practice" to share ideas and products among
              the conference participants and participants and program leadership.  ***
