Abstract    :
                   This proposal concerns a one-year program of research  into nuclear
              reaction dynamics at intermediate and high  energies.  The research, which will
              be conducted primarily  at Lawrence University, will focus mainly on the
              nuclear  equation of state, response of particle interactions to the  nuclear
              environment, collective directed and radial flow,  resonance production,
              propagation, and decay, features of  phase transitions in nuclear matter,
              relativistic quantum  field theories at zero and finite temperature, non- 
              equilibrium effects, electromagnetic radiation from nuclear  and sub-nuclear
              degrees of freedom and dynamical aspects of  quark-gluon plasma.
