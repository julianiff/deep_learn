Abstract    :
              The focus of this work will be to cross-couple 3-ene-1-yne intermediates   using
              palladium catalysis.  The resulting living polymer will lead to the  
              production of ring-expanded radialenes or the evolution of a tubular   helix
              structure. The properties of both of the potential products are of   interest.
              With this Small Grant for Exploratory Research (SGER) award,   the Synthetic
              Organic Program is supporting the research of Dr. Eric D.   Edstrom of the
              Department of Chemistry at the University of Montana.     Professor Edstrom
              will test the hypothesis that unsaturated hydrocarbon   units can be induced to
              polymerize to afford tubular helix structures.    The resulting polymers are
              expected to show interesting physical   properties.
