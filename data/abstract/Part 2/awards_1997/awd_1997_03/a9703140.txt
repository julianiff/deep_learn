Abstract    :
                   This grant will support the Particle Data Center at  Lawrence Berkeley
              Laboratory.  This group is responsible for  collecting the results of many
              different particle physics  experiments and providing them in summary form. 
              They also  make this information available on the Web.  In addition,  they
              provide an interactive Web site for the general public  which allows anyone
              interested in Particle Physics to  explore many of its concepts.
