Abstract    :
                The PI requests support to provide a summer research experience for 15
              minority and women scientists at the Scripps Institution of Oceanography. 
              These NSF-funded students will be in addition to ten other students funded by
              SIO that are part of the summer research program.  Summer students will work
              for 10 weeks with a faculty mentor from SIO on research projects in one of the
              marine science disciplines.  Students will also attend various workshops and
              seminars during the course of the summer, both on scientific issues and
              professional development.
