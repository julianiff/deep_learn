Abstract    :
              9711008  Rudnick           This award provides partial funding for the support
              of a  technician who will take charge of the operation and maintenance of  the
              inductively coupled plasma-mass spectrometer (ICP-MS)  laboratory in the
              Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences at  Harvard University. The ICP-MS
              laboratory at Harvard is a research  laboratory providing multi-element
              analysis capabilities for a  variety of research groups in geochemistry,
              atmospheric chemistry,  and archeology.   ***
