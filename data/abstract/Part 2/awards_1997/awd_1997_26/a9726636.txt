Abstract    :
                This award supports Dr. Peter Winkler and a graduate student from the
              University of   Nevada-Reno to collaborate with L .S. Cederbaum of the
              Department of Theoretical   Chemistry at the University of Heidelberg.  The
              collaboration will develop propagator   theory for atomic and molecular
              structure calculations as well as for scattering problems.    The research
              focuses on atomic calculations with a novel multi-channel propagator  
              approach.  The research is likely to lead to many interesting applications of
              the new   formalism to the study of atomic processes in various environments. 
              Working with the   German group will give Dr. Winkler's group access to a
              considerable reservoir of   knowledge and computational expertise.
