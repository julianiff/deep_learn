Abstract    :
                   Advanced Study Institute on the Techniques and Concepts  of High Energy
              Physics is designed to educate promising  particle physicists at an early stage
              in their career.  The  concentration is on the latest in experimental tools. 
              This  Institute also receives partial support from NATO for its  enhancement of
              exchanges among scientists if different  countries.
