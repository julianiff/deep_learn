Abstract    :
                This project will study the existence and multiplicity of radial  and
              nonradial solutions to some nonlinear elliptic boundary value  proplems.  In
              particular the nonlinear Dirichlet problem when the  nonlinearity is
              superlinear, with jumping, and at the critical  exponent.
