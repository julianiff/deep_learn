Abstract    :
              This grant will support the renovation and update of the  University of Delaware
              Bartol Research Institute Computing  Facility.  This facility is used to
              support research in the  broad fields of space physics fluid dynamics, and high
               energy air showers.  The computational speed and capability  of the facility
              will be upgraded, and graphics visualization  applications will be developed. 
              This activity is supported jointly by the MPS Office of  Multidisciplinary
              Activities (`OMA`) and the MPS Division of  Physics.
