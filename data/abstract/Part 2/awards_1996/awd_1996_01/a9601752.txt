Abstract    :
                   Several theoretical investigations into processes of high  energy atomic
              physics are undertaken to elucidate the  interactions of photons with
              correlated atomic structures.  The  study yields a better understanding of
              relativistic, retardation  and higher multipole effects in regimes where they
              become  comparable or dominate electron correlation effects.  A number of  the
              proposed studies will be useful in the interpretation of  experiments being
              carried out at third generation light sources.
