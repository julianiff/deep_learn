Abstract    :
              A dusty plasma, containing negatively charged, micron sized, dust  grains in
              addition to the Usual collection of ions and electrons,  is studied using a
              Q-machine modified to levitate the dust  grains.  The laboratory work focuses
              on the steady state  properties of the dusty plasma, waves in the dusty plasma
              and  plasma instabilities created by the dust grains.
