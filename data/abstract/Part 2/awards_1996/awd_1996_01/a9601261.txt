Abstract    :
                   The research focuses on the quantitative modeling of  Bose condensed,
              trapped atomic systems.  Analytic and  numerical techniques examine the effect
              of the geometry of  the trap, the kinetics of condensate formation, the nature 
              of the phase transition and the effect of particle  correlations.
