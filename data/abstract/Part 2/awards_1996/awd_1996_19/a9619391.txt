Abstract    :
                This project involves a planning conference to combine archaeologists,
              cultural and physical anthropologists to discuss a research agenda for the
              Human Genome Diversity The results of this conference will stimulate research
              across the sub-disciplines of anthropology, and serve to integrate the field,
              as well as advance our research capacity in the broad area of studying the
              social, cultural and genetic significance of human diversity
