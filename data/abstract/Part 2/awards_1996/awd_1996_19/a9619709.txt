Abstract    :
              Dr. Mary K. Boyd and other members of the Chemistry Department at Loyola  
              University of Chicago are being supported to establish a Research   Experiences
              for Undergraduates (REU) site in chemistry.  For the period   1997-99, nine
              undergraduate students will spend ten weeks each summer   actively engaged in
              basic research projects drawn from the traditional   areas of chemistry.  In
              addition, field trips are scheduled to nearby   industrial and government
              research facilities.  Participants are   recruited from the Chicago
              metropolitan area.  The recruitment emphasis   is on students who might not
              otherwise choose a career in chemistry.
