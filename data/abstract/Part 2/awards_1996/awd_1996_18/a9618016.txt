Abstract    :
              Workshop:  When and How Can You Specify a Probability Distribution When You
              Don't Know Much - II  This proposal seeks funds to conduct a second workshop on
              the selection and use of probability distributions by industry, government and
              regulatory agencies for the purpose of conducting risk assessments.Objectives
              are  to further assess progress in selecting input distributions and
              characterize the state of the art in assessment techniques used by regulatory
              agencies.
