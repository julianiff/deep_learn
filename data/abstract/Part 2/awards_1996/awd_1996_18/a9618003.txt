Abstract    :
              `Strategic Policy Innovation and Social Learning:  Flood Hazard Mitigation,
              Recycling, and Air Quality in Tulsa, OK           The PI proposes to study
              three environmental policy innovations in Tulsa, OK.  The  purpose of this
              research is to investigate the mechanisms that led to these three 
              implementations and to determine the adequacy of current theory to explain
              their  success.  Hypotheses pertaining to the following topics will be
              examined:  (1) the role  of scientific and technical information; (2)
              development and influence of advocacy  coalitions; (3) effect of social
              learning on attainment of policy goals; (4) strategic roles  of policy
              entrepreneurs.
