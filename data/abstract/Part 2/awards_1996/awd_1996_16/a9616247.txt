Abstract    :
              The foci of this research are two-fold.  First, the investigation of the  
              chemistry of cyclooctadienyliron complexes will lead to stereo- and  
              regiocontrolled functionalization of this important carbocyclic ring   system. 
              Second,  the scope of iron-mediated cyclocarbonylation alpha,   omega-diynes
              leading to the construction of bicyclo(5.3.0)decanes will be   investigated. 
              With this award, the Synthetic Organic Program is   supporting the research of
              Dr. Anthony J. Pearson of the Department of   Chemistry at Case Western Reserve
              University.  Professor Pearson will   focus his work on investigating the
              applications of organoiron chemistry   to the construction of natural products
              having important biological   properties.
