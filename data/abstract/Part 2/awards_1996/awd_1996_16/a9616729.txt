Abstract    :
              Cohen, Judith  AST 96-16729    Dr. Cohen will analyze existent spectroscopic
              data sets  from the Keck telescope.  These include observations of  about 200
              globular clusters around M87.  The study will  derive the abundances and
              kinematics of the M87 globular  system and further the knowledge of the
              rotation,  metallicity and velocity dispersion of the M87 halo.
