Abstract    :
              Valley Forge Christian College requests support from NSF to connect it's campus
              to the Internet.  The College provides bible and religious education degrees
              with a student population 500 students with 19 full time and 11 part time
              faculty.  The College  intends to connect to the Internet through the Voicenet,
              a local Internet service provider.  The campus needs the Internet connection to
              be able to access information resources throughout the state, the nation and
              the world.  The connection will benefit the faculty, staff students of this
              institution as well as the community in general.  The award provides partial
              support of the project for two years.
