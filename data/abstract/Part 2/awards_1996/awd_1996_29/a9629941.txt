Abstract    :
              This award from the Analytical and Surface Chemistry Program of the  Division of
              Chemistry and the Solid State Chemistry and Polymers  Program of the Division
              of Materials Research provides partial  support to enable the participation of
              ten to twelve graduate  students and postdoctoral researchers in the 1996
              Gordon Research  Conference on Organic Thin Films.  This conference brings
              together  leading researchers from diverse scientific backgrounds to address 
              topics at the intellectual frontiers of this fundamentally exciting  and
              technologically important multidisciplinary area.   Participation in this
              conference by the targeted young researchers  will provide them with a unique
              opportunity to gain deep insights  and broad perspectives in this rapidly
              evolving field.  This award enables the participation of young researchers in
              the  1996 Gordon Research Conference on Organic Thin Films.   Developments in
              this area hold promise for broad fundamental and  technological impacts.
