Abstract    :
                   Theoretical studies of the interaction of laser pulses with  the atomic
              media are pursued using high speed computers to  elucidate the nonlinear
              quantum dynamics of the interaction  process.  Major parts of the program
              involve significant  undergraduate participation in a developing computational
              physics  program.
