Abstract    :
              9633779  Pederson    The purpose of this project is to organize and manage the
              1996  National EPSCoR Conference to be held in the Washington DC-area in 
              September of 1996.  The two-day conference will include speakers,  poster
              sessions, and discussions on a variety of topics of  importance to the EPSCoR
              state participants.  Travel grants will be  awarded for up to five individuals
              from each EPSCoR state who are  key contributors to the EPSCoR program in that
              state.
