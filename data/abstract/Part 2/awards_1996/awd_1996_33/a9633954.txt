Abstract    :
                  This action funds a graduate student travel award; this is a  supporting
              activity of the NSF Minority Postdoctoral  Research Fellowships as announced in
              NSF 94-133.    This travel award will permit the applicant to travel to the 
              United Kingdom, Spain, and Portugal where she will meet with  leading
              authorities on harvester ants, the subject of her  doctoral research project. 
              She will discuss the possibility  of postdoctoral research in one of these
              laboratories upon  completion of her PhD in June, 1997.
