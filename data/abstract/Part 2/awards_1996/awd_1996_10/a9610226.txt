              9610226  Carignan  This award to Skidaway Institute of Oceanography  provides
              shipboard technical support (as required), shore-based support, instrument
              maintenance and instrument calibration for researchers using R/V Blue Fin, a
              research vessel operated as part of the University National Oceanographic
              Laboratory System research fleet.  The technical support awarded here will
              assist NSF-funded ocean researchers conduct studies in the North Atlantic Ocean
              in 1997, and will emphasize studies of coastal oceanography along the Southeast
              United States.  ***
