Abstract    :
                   This grant provides partial support to participants in the  Workshop New
              Directions for High Energy Physics planned by the  Division of Particles and
              Fields (DPF) and the Division of  Physics of Beams (DPB) of the American
              Physical Society (APS).  The Workshop will be held June 25 through July 12 this
              year in  Snowmass, Colorado.  The goal of the Workshop is to `...begin to 
              develop a coherent plan for the long term future of the U.S. high  energy
              physics program in the LHC (Large Hadron Collider) era.`
