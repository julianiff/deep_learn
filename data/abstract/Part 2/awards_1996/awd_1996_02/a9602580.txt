Abstract    :
                   This will be a joint Indo-U.S. workshop on string theory to  be held at the
              Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Bombay for  August 29-31, 1996.  The
              purpose of the workshop is to discuss  recent developments in string theory. 
              The topics to be covered  include duality symmetries in string theory,
              non-critical  strings, black holes, and gauge theory.
