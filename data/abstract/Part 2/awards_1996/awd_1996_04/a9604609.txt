Abstract    :
                   One of the highest priority areas in elementary  particles physics is 
              experiments that test the standard  model through the symmetry between
              particles and anti  particles known as CP symmetry.  This conference is
              designed  to study recent advances in this area as they relate to the  b-quark,
              predicted to be one of the best tools for such  studies.
