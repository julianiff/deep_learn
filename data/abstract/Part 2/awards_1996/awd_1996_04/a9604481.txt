              Abstract  EEC-9604481  Alden S. Bean  Lehigh University      This award funds a
              one-year phase out of the Lehigh University Industry/University Cooperative
              Research Center for Innovation Management.  Also, two years of support for an
              evaluator to study the industry/university interactions occurring during the
              Center's phase out and one year after to study the transitions to a
              disciplinary program.  The Center continues to meet the criteria for renewal of
              the Industry/University Cooperative Research Centers (I/UCRC) Program.  ***
