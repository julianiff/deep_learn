Abstract    :
              With this Alan T. Waterman Award, the Foundation is supporting the   research of
              Dr. Robert M. Waymouth of the Department of Chemistry at   Stanford University.
               Professor Waymouth is the twenty-first recipient of   the award and will
              receive a medal as well as a $500,000 grant over three   years for scientific
              research or advanced study in any field of science.    Dr. Waymouth has been
              cited for his seminal contributions to the design   of well-defined
              organometallic catalysts for the synthesis of novel   polymers.  His research
              interests target the interface of inorganic,   organic and polymer chemistry.  
              Dr. Waymouth's dedication to teaching is   attested to by his award winning
              activities at Stanford.
