Abstract    :
                9615642    This proposal is to fund partial travel expenses for seven U.S.
              researchers to participate in an international conference on Computational Heat
              Transfer. The Conference is organized by the International Centre for Heat and
              Mass Transfer and will take place in Cesme, Turkey in May, 1997.  A wide range
              of disciplines will be represented, including mechanical and aerospace
              engineering, chemical engineering, atmospheric and oceanographic science,
              physics, mathematics, and computer science.
