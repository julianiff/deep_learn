Abstract    :
              Antonucci, Robert  AST 96-17160    Dr. Antonucci will conduct a number of
              related research  projects bearing on the nature of active galactic nuclei, 
              primeval galaxies and the intergalactic medium.  The first  group of projects
              exploits observations with optical  spectropolarimetry on hidden AGN.  The
              second group  describes some tests of optical/UV and IR emission  mechanisms. 
              The additional projects will use the Lick  adaptive optics system with laser
              guide stars, attempts to  detect the ionized intergalactic medium and a search
              for  ultrahigh redshift sources.
