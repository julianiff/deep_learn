Abstract    :
              Shull, Michael  AST 96-17073    Dr. Shull will study the absorption line spectra
              of QSOs  using rigorous models of radiative transfer, gas dynamics,  and
              thermal and chemical evolution coupled to modern  cosmology.  There are three
              main topics -- the temporal  evolution and spatial variation of the
              metagalactic  radiation field, the effects of local sources and the  nature of
              the low-redshift Lyman-alpha clouds and galaxy  halos.
