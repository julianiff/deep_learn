Abstract    :
              Modeling the Production of Quality                                              
                                                                                             
                                   The PIs propose to investigate the trade-offs that may
              occur in the management of  quality.  Increasing the quality of a product or
              service may have negative ramifications,  such as increasing the delivery time,
              for example.  The PIs intend to develop quantitative models which will examine
              these trade-offs and then run computational studies in order to test these
              models.  One goal of this research is to provide information and support for
              managerial decision-making in the areas of implementation and execution of
              quality management programs.  The PIs hope to provide insights to both the
              manufacturing and service industries.
