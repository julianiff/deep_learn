                                          Form 10 Abstract    Proposal AST 96-17619  PI: 
              Robert Milkey  Institution:  American Astronomical Society      This award
              supports travel expenses for astronomers to attend the  1997 meeting of the
              International Astronomical Union in Kyoto,  Japan, during 18-30 August, 1997.
              This triennial meeting is the  premier international meeting for astronomers.
              In addition to the  regular sessions, many of the topical commissions also
              meet,  establishing new standards and evaluating the status of research  in
              various fields.
