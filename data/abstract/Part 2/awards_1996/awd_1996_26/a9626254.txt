Abstract    :
              9626254  Cooper      Carlow College requests support from NSF for a connection
              to PREPNet.  This grant will be used to help the institution get access to the
              Internet.  PREPnet, a midlevel network in Pennsylvania, will provide operations
              management and information  services and it provides access to the Internet, a
              collection of interoperable networks connecting most research and education
              organizations in the United States.  The Pennsylvania college need the Internet
              connections to be able to access information resources throughout the state,
              the nation and the world.  The connection will benefit the faculty, staff
              students of Carlow College as well as the community in general.
