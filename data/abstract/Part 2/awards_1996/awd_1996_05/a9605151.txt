Abstract    :
                This award supports a planning visit to India by Dr. Mahadev Bhat, from the
              Department of Environmental Studies at Florida International University, to
              visit Dr. Anil Gupta, of the Indian Institute of Management, Dr. K. N. Ninan,
              of the Center for Ecological Studies at the Institute for Social and Economic
              Change, and with local government officials in Ahmedabad and Bangalore, for the
              purpose of developing a research project on the causes and resolutions of
              multi-tier conflicts associated with changing global intellectual property
              rights for biotechnology.  Technology's impact on how people work and increases
              in international economic endeavors cause changes to intellectual property
              rights.
