Abstract    :
                   Accurate calculations on the electron affinities of a  variety of atomic
              negative ions are carried out using  relativistic configuration interaction and
              relativistic many-  body perturbation methods.  The results are useful in the 
              interpretation of the spectra and electrons emission of  these ions.
