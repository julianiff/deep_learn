Abstract    :
                   Theoretical and computational studies of the  photoionization of highly
              excited (hollow) atoms are carried  out using a combination of saddle point and
              complex rotation  techniques.  The numerical results provide the most accurate 
              values for the energies and widths available for these  complex atomic systems.
