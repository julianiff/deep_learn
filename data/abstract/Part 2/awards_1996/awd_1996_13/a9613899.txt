Abstract    :
                The Community College of Philadelphia requests support from NSF  to connect
              its campus to the Internet.  This grant will be used  to help the institution
              get access to the Internet.  VoiceNet, a  local service provider, will provided
              the the lines used to  access to the Internet.    The Community College of
              Philadelphia needs the Internet  connection to be able to access information
              resources throughout  the state, the nation and the world.  The connection will
              benefit  the faculty, staff students of this institution as well as the 
              community in general.
