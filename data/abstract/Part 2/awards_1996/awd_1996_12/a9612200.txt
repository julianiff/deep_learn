Abstract    :
              A joint experimental/theoretical program is undertaken in  order to study a new
              class of microcavity resonators having  whispering gallery resonances.  The
              Q-values of these  asymmetric cavities can be controlled at will and allow 
              fundamental studies of quantum/wave chaos to be performed as  well as having
              the potential to impact the development of  optical communications networks.
