Abstract    :
              This project is to conduct the US-PRC Researcher Exchange Program on behalf of
              the NSF Earthquake Hazard Mitigation Program, as envisioned in the Annex III of
              the US/PRC Protocol for Scientific and Technical Cooperative Research in
              Earthquake Studies, Earthquake Engineering and Hazard Mitigation.  The project
              will provide financial support for travel and administrative and educational
              infrastructure to undertake logistical planning and coordination of the
              scientific exchange visits of the US and PRC investigators.
