Abstract    :
              The focus of this research is the preparationof thioquinones,  
              chalcogenofulvenes, chalcogenofulvalenes , nonclassical thiophenes and  
              thiophene-based macrocycles.  Results will lead to a better understanding   of
              the properties of such compounds and subsequent application to the   design of
              organic materials in the area of synthetic metals,   superconductors and
              materials with nonlinear optical properties.  With   this award, the Synthetic
              Organic Program is supporting the research of   Dr. Michael P. Cava of the
              Department of Chemistry at the University of   Alabama.  Professor Cava will
              focus his work on the preparation of   compounds that have the potential to
              lead to application in the areas of   synthetic metals, conducting polymers and
              nonlinear optics.
