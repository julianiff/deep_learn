Abstract    :
              The research objective is to determine the practicality of  the optical control
              of chemical reactions in the gas phase  and on surfaces using short, <1
              picosecond, laser pulses.  The methods to be employed bypass the early
              difficulties of  laser chemistry using temporally short pulses and pump-probe 
              techniques and extend the notions to surfaces and chiral  molecules.
