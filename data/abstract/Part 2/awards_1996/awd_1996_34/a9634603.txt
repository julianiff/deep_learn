Abstract    :
              With this award, the Synthetic Organic Program supports a request by   Barry M.
              Trost of the Department of Chemistry at Stanford University to    provide
              symposium costs for the William S. Johnson Memorial Symposium.    The
              symposium, honoring the memory of one of the true icons of organic   chemistry,
              will take place at the American Chemical Society Meeting    which will be held
              in San Francisco, California on April 13-17, 1997
