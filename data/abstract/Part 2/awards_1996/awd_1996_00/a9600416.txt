Abstract    :
              The interaction of positrons with molecules is investigated using  crossed
              beams.  The energies and masses of the dissociation  fragments are measured
              using mass spectrometric and kinetic  energy spectrometry in order to study the
              chemical stability of  molecules containing positronium.  Applications to solid
              state  physics, medical physics, polymer research and astrophysics are 
              anticipated.
