Abstract    :
              The 1996 Ultrafast Phenomena Topical Meeting will be held May 28-  June 1 in
              Coronado, California.  This conference will feature a  series of presentations
              on the latest developments in the field  of ultrafast laser science,
              emphasizing both technological  developments and basic seientific studies. 
              Topics in the program  cover a broad range of multidisciplinary topics in areas
              of  chemistry, biology, physics, and materials science.
