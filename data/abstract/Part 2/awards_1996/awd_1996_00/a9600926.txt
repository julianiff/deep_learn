Abstract    :
                   The principal focus of this research is to carry out  high precision
              calculations on the lithium and beryllium  atoms using highly correlated atomic
              wavefunctions.  To  accomplish this, it is necessary to develop sophisticated 
              techniques for the calculation of the electron-electron  repulsion integrals
              which appear in multi-electron atoms.
