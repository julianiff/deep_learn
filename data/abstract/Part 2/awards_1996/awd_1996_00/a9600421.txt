Abstract    :
                   A cooperative research program on the development of  novel theoretical
              techniques (`nonperturbative expansions`)  primarily applied to quantum
              chromodynamics, the theory of  strong nuclear interactions, will be carried
              out.  There  will be collaboration between a researcher at the University  of
              Oklahoma and one at the Bogoliubov Laboratory at Dubna,  Russia.  The
              investigators have been working individually on  closely related work.  Their
              collaboration will facilitate a  better understanding of the theory of strong
              interactions.
