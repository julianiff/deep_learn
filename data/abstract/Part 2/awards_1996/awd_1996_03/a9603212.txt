Abstract    :
              This research combines the PI's expertise in metal ion coordination chemistry
              with that of   his collaborator in organic supramolecular structures based on
              coordination chemistry.    The foreign PI's group has been a leader in Europe
              in this field.  Raymond is at the   forefront of supramolecular chemistry, and
              is known worldwide for his work in   siderophore chemistry.  This particular
              area of self-assembly and rational design based on   symmetry considerations
              will undoubtedly provide new chemistry for the future.  The   collaboration
              involves several graduate and postdoctoral students, all of whom will benefit  
              from the collaboration.
