Abstract    :
              The proposed research investigates high-tech ceramics based on crystalline
              phases in the   Si-Al-Y-O-N system.  Better knowledge of their thermodynamic
              properties could lead to   the synthesis of lower-cost materials with better
              properties.  This project combines the   expertise of the calorimetry group at
              Princeton with the materials and processing group in   Stuttgart.  The
              Stuttgart group will synthesize and characterize samples, while the   Princeton
              group performs high-temperature solution calorimetry to obtain enthalpies of  
              formation of the crystals.  The data derived will be incorporated into
              databases and used   to calculate phase equilibria, crystalline paths, and
              crystal-chemical correlations.
