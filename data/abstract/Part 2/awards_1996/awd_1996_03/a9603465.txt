Abstract    :
              The general objective of the proposed research is to gain an improved
              understanding of   high-dimensional convex bodies, with special emphasis on
              algorithmic aspects of the   convex bodies that arise in applications such as
              mathematical programming and computer   science.  For years the close
              collaboration between the PI and Peter Gritzman of the   University of Trier,
              Germany has produced results in the areas of mathematical   optimization,
              discrete mathematics, and computer science, among others.  This proposal  
              seeks to include students on both sides of the Atlantic in this collaboration
              to establish   new contacts and open up new areas of investigation.
