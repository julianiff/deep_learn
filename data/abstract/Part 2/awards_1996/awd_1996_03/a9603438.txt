Abstract    :
                This three-year award supports U.S.-France cooperative research in plant
              molecular   biology between Julian T. Schroeder of the University of California
              at San Diego and   Raoul Ranjeva of the University Paul Sabatier in Toulouse,
              France.  The award provides   travel support to Dr. Schroeder and a graduate
              student.  The objective of the research is   to improve knowledge of the
              mechanism by which calcium influx is triggered in plant cell   signaling.   It
              adds an international dimension to an existing NSF grant and takes   advantage
              of the U.S. and French investigators' complementary expertise.
