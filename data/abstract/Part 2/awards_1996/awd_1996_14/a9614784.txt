Abstract    :
              The Pacific Software Research Institute is holding a summer  school on advanced
              functional programming techniques.  The school  is designed to be of use to
              university faculty, industry and  government workers interested in software
              technology.  It will  also bring together a group of leading researchers in
              advanced  functional programming to lecture and interact with the other 
              participants.  This will allow in-depth discussion by both  researchers and
              practitioners in the field, providing an  assessment of the state of the art
              and new directions of research  needed.
